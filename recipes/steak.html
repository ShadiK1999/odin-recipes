Medium Rare Steak

Description

Everybody loves the kind of dinner that you can cook without a recipe. The truth is, good cooking is more about technique than recipes and the best dishes are often the simplest to prepare. A properly cooked steak is case in point. With just a few ingredients and a single pan, you can cook a steak that’s as delicious as one you’d order in a high-end steakhouse.

Ingredients

  • 2 New York strip or ribeye steaks
  • 1 heaping teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • a few sprigs frsh thyme leaves

Steps

  1. Season steaks with salt and pepper
  2. Turn on exchaust fan and heat a heavy pan
  3. add oil to the pan and heat until it begins to shimmer and move fluidly around the pan
  4. Carefully set the steaks in the pan for about 3 minutes
  5. Flip steaks and allow to cook for 3 to 4 minutes on the second side
  6. During the last minute of cooking, add the butter and thyme to the pan with the steaks
  7. Transfer steak to plates and serve them hot
  8. Serve with sides of your choice
